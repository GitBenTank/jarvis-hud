## 2. Set a provisional price (say it out loud)

Pick **one** default you can say without flinching; adjust per thread.

| Shape | When | Number to try |
|-------|------|----------------|
| **Design-partner pilot** | Easier yeses, reference / case-study trade | **~$15k** fixed, scoped |
| **Fixed pilot** | Target is already security-serious; workflow is materially real | **$25k+** fixed, scoped |

Add calendar, scope boundary, and what “done” means in **one sentence** when you name the number.

---

## 3. Send the warm note

- **Template:** [governed-agent-pilot-vp-eng-warm-outreach.md](./governed-agent-pilot-vp-eng-warm-outreach.md) — **edit in your voice** (not brochure tone).
- **Deadline:** today if possible, **tomorrow at latest**—do not wait for a perfect deck footer or blessed PDF.

---

## 4. Book two to three conversations

- **Goal is not** to close everyone.
- **Goal is** to learn **where the offer bites** and **where it stalls** (workflow fit, price, security path, “not now”).
