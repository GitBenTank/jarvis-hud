# Jarvis

## The control plane for AI **execution**

**Propose → approve → execute → receipt → trace**

---

# The shift

## Agents are moving from **generation** to **action**

- Sending email and messages
- Changing systems and configuration
- Triggering workflows and integrations

**The question is no longer only “what did it say?”**  
It is **“what did it do?”**

---

# The problem

## Execution is not **controlled**

- Side effects can run **without explicit human authorization**
- **Approval** is often conflated with **execution** — or skipped
- **Logs** record activity; they are not **proof** of who authorized what

**Runtime risk is unbounded authority, not bad copy.**

---

# Market validation

## Enterprises are investing in **agent infrastructure**

- Registries and catalogs — *what exists, who owns it*
- Orchestration and platforms — *how agents are wired*
- Governance layers — *policy, visibility, reuse*

**This proves the category. It does not close the execution gap.**

---

# The gap

## **Visibility ≠ control**

Platforms answer: *What can be discovered and shared?*

They do not, by themselves, answer:

> *What may happen **at the moment** an agent acts — under whose authority — with what proof?*

```
Agents & tools
      ↓
Platforms (catalogs, orchestration)
      ↓
   [ execution void ]
      ↓
Production systems
```

---

# Jarvis

## **Approval + proof** for every action

| Step | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Propose** | Agent or connector submits an explicit action |
| **Approve** | Human gate — authorization is deliberate |
| **Execute** | **Separate** step — approval is not execution |
| **Receipt** | Artifact + outcome record |
| **Trace** | Reconstruct the story end-to-end |

**Agents can propose anything. Authority lives in the control plane.**

# Why now

## Three forces at once

1. **Capability** — tool-using agents are productized, not experimental
2. **Adoption** — agents touch production systems and real credentials
3. **Pressure** — governance and risk frameworks expect **accountability**, not vibes

**The missing layer is execution control with evidence.**

---

# Why this wins

## **Agent-agnostic** control plane

- Sits between **intent** (models, frameworks, connectors) and **side effects**
- Enforces **authority boundaries** — who may cause what
- **Receipts + traces** for audit, dispute, and operations — not log archaeology

**Not competing to be the catalog. Competing to be the moment of execution.**

---

# Vision

## Every production AI action is **governed**

Jarvis becomes the:

- **Control** layer — authorization before irreversible change
- **Audit** layer — proof, not inference from logs
- **Trust** layer — humans remain the trust root for action
